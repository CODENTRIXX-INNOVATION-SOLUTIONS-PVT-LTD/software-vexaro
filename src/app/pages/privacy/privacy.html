Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

  • Vexaro Courier Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is committed to protecting your data
  • This policy covers: platform users, shipment recipients, and website visitors
  • Applies to all data collected through the Vexaro platform and website

2. What Data We Collect

From Distributors & Merchants (account holders):

  • Name, business name, email, phone number
  • GST number, business address
  • Bank/UPI details (for COD payouts and wallet top-ups)
  • Login activity, device and IP information

From Shipment bookings:

  • Sender details (warehouse address, contact)
  • Consignee details (name, address, phone, email)
  • Parcel details (weight, dimensions, item description, value)
  • AWB numbers, tracking events, delivery timestamps

From the website:

  • Contact form submissions (name, email, message, team type)
  • Cookies and analytics data (page visits, session data)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your Vexaro account
  • To process shipment bookings with carrier partners
  • To calculate and deduct wallet charges accurately
  • To generate shipping labels and AWB numbers
  • To send shipment status notifications (SMS/WhatsApp/email)
  • To resolve weight disputes and compensation claims
  • To comply with Indian tax and GST regulations
  • To improve platform features and performance
  • To send important service updates (not marketing without consent)

4. Who We Share Data With

Carrier partners (DTDC, Delhivery, Ekart, Bluedart, etc.):

  • Consignee name, address, phone — required for delivery
  • Parcel details — required for pickup and transit

Payment processors:

  • For wallet top-up transactions only
  • We do not store full card details

Government/regulatory bodies:

  • When required by Indian law (GST, court orders, etc.)

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use consignee data for any purpose other than delivery

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained for the duration of account + 5 years (GST compliance)
  • Shipment records: 5 years from shipment date
  • COD and wallet records: 7 years (financial audit requirement)
  • Website enquiry data: 1 year
  • You can request deletion of non-mandatory data after account closure

6. Consignee Data (End Customer)

  • Consignee data (your customer's details) is provided by the Merchant
  • Vexaro processes it only for delivery purposes
  • Consignees can request their data by contacting support
  • Vexaro does not contact consignees for any purpose other than delivery

7. Data Security

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords stored using bcrypt hashing (never plain text)
  • JWT tokens expire every 24 hours
  • Access to data is role-restricted (Merchant cannot see Distributor data, etc.)
  • Regular security audits conducted
  • Data stored on secure cloud infrastructure within India

8. Cookies

  • Essential cookies: required for login sessions
  • Analytics cookies: page performance (can be opted out)
  • No advertising or third-party tracking cookies
  • Cookie preference can be managed in browser settings

9. Your Rights

Under Indian IT Act and DPDP Act 2023:

  • Right to access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to correction — update inaccurate data
  • Right to deletion — request deletion of non-mandatory data
  • Right to withdraw consent — for marketing communications
  • To exercise rights: email privacy@vexaro.in

10. Children's Privacy

  • Platform is for business use only, not intended for persons under 18
  • We do not knowingly collect data from minors

11. Contact for Privacy

  • Data Protection Officer: privacy@vexaro.in
  • Registered address: Vexaro Courier Solutions Pvt. Ltd., Indore, Madhya Pradesh
  • Response time: within 7 working days

12. Changes to This Policy

  • Policy may be updated to reflect legal or product changes
  • Users will be notified via email 14 days before changes take effect
  • Continued use = acceptance of updated policy.
