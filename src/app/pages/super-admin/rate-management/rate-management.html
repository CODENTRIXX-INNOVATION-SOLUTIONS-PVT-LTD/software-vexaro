Logistics Rates Formula

Platform system charges are dynamically computed based on active pricing sheets:

Total Shipping Cost = Base Rate + (Additional Weight Units × Addl. Rate) + Fuel Surcharge (%)
  • Base rate values cover the initial manifest slab (0.5kg by default).
  • Fuel percentages stack atop the weight billing sum.
  • COD Cash charges are calculated dynamically upon package booking.
